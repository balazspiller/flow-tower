Flow system

  • Horizontal speed increases jump power and lets you skip floors.
  • Landing on higher platforms quickly keeps the combo alive.
  • At combo x5+, the tower enters flow: trails, speed lines, stronger sound, and extra jump assist. At x20+, jumps get extra hang time and overdrive effects.
  • Rare blue hourglasses slow time, yellow springs launch you upward, and violet beacons pop extra platforms into the tower.
  • Above height 500, rare spiky ledges can be passed through from below but end the run if you drop onto them.
  • Perfect-ish landings preserve momentum better than hard landings.
