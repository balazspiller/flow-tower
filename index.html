Flow system

  • Horizontal speed increases jump power and lets you skip floors.
  • The tower has side walls. Hold the opposite direction as you hit one to rebound back into the climb.
  • Landing on higher platforms quickly keeps the combo alive.
  • Every 1000 height starts a new tower level: the platform tint changes, spacing relaxes, and the climb ramps up again.
  • At combo x5+, the tower enters flow: trails, speed lines, stronger sound, and extra jump assist. At x20+, jumps get extra hang time and overdrive effects.
  • Rare blue hourglasses slow time, yellow springs launch you upward, and violet beacons pop extra platforms into the tower.
  • Above height 500, cracked platforms crumble shortly after landing. Above height 750, rare spiky ledges can be passed through from below but end the run if you drop onto them. Above height 1000, moving platforms sweep between the tower walls. Above height 1500, blinking platforms phase in and out.
  • Perfect-ish landings preserve momentum better than hard landings.
